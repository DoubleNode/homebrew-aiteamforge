The situation: iOS and Android widgets need real-time balance updates. Our current polling approach works, but it's not sustainable.

*Reviews the options*

Here's what I'm seeing:

**Option A: Firestore Realtime Listeners**
- Pros: True real-time, built-in offline support
- Cons: Higher read costs, connection management complexity
- Cost estimate: $200/month additional

**Option B: Cloud Functions with Push Notifications**
- Pros: Lower cost, more control
- Cons: Not truly real-time, more moving parts
- Cost estimate: $50/month additional

**Option C: Hybrid Approach**
- Realtime for active widgets
- Push notifications for background updates
- Cost estimate: $100/month additional

I've made my decision. We go with Option C.

The hybrid approach gives us the best of both worlds while keeping costs manageable. Sometimes you have to find the balance—pun intended.

O'Brien, start implementing the infrastructure. Dax, review the data model. I'll handle the cross-team communication."
